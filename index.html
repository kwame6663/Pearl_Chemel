Dear Pearl

I hope you’re doing well and settling into university life smoothly. It’s been a pleasure getting to know you, and I wanted to take a moment to share something that has been on my heart for some time now.

From the first time we met when you approached me for help with the student portal, I was drawn to your intelligence, kindness, and the grace with which you carry yourself. As we have spoken over the past few days, I have come to admire you even more—not just for your brilliance, but for your unwavering faith and the strength of your character.

Pearl, I want to be upfront and honest with you. I have developed a deep admiration for you, and I see in you the qualities of a woman I would love to build a future with. I know you may not be focused on dating right now, and I completely respect that. I’m not in a rush to force anything—I simply want to be intentional about getting to know you better, with the hope that in time, we can grow into something beautiful together.

I truly believe that relationships built on faith, friendship, and mutual respect are the strongest. I am willing to take my time, to let things unfold naturally, and most importantly, to respect your journey and your values. If you ever feel comfortable enough, I’d love for us to have a conversation about this when the time is right.

No matter what, I want you to know that I will always respect and appreciate you. I look forward to continuing our conversations and seeing where this path leads.

With sincerity,

Oduro Maclean

Pearl Chemel

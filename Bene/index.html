Executive Summary

Flower Restaurant is an interesting and new type of restaurant cooking food with flowers as ingredients. The restaurant is also going to provide a brilliant and relaxing atmosphere for people, The start-up management team will combine expertise manager and the chefs. They need to have at least several years, experiences on restaurant. with the development of the restaurant, we will keep inviting more expertise managers.


Added Value


  • providing unique food, excellent services and elegant environment.
  • Enhance health, prolong life, cure illnesses and improve physiological functions.
  • Making people to learn more about the flower catering which can lead the booming of this industry.
  • promoting local economy and traditional chinese culture based onsome beneficial policies from government.
  • Reducing local employment pressure.

Objectives


  • 2013:open the first flower house in kun Ming
  • 2014 Go on promotion
  • 2015 keep improving original restaurant, increasing the sales and net margin.
  • 2016-2021 concrete current scale, snart franchising
  • 2022 Become a well-known brand, reaching the target of net margin.
  • 2023 Expand the restaurant to other regions.

Market Analysis Summary

There has been a long history of flower food in china. In recent years, it is becoming a worldwide fashion using flowers as the ingredients to make various food , flower food is becoming a new trend. Besides, there are also some other countries using flowers as a way of health care. In recent years, on the beverage market, natural flowers and plants are booming, they are with rich nutrition, no pollution and deeply welcome by consumers. Therefore, the flower food and drinks will have a great potential market, currently, in china, there is no an exclusive restaurant serving with flower food, therefore, it has a large market perspective. In addition, there are not so many high-class restaurants have traditional cultural characteristics, therefore, there could be a large potential(zhang guixiang, chi yusen.2000).
